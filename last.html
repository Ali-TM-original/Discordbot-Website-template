Our Story!

Ami was made just for fun, when i downloaded discord and i found & read the API, and it started as a private bot for a server of friends. Later i decided to apply more time on it, and make it public (ps: i didn't know a shit about development, i just started for fun). After 2 weeks it start looks a good and enjoyable bot, and i was really proud about me and Ami, so i decided to publish it on a bot list (top.gg if i remember), and from that ami got 250 guilds in less than 24h.. amazing! I was like "wtf is happening", and yes, after that, i just needed to wait 3 weeks to apply for the bot verification, and in this 3 weeks, i worked so hard on ami, adding like 40 new commands (3 weeks.. no social life lmao). And yes, now i'm here, telling you to give a chanche to her, and help me to make Ami one of the most used bot on discord!
